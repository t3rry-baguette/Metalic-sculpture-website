About Us

Master craftsmanship in metal sculpture

The Moses Sculptures Story

Moses Sculptures is a premium artisan workshop based in Dagoretti, Nairobi, specializing exclusively in handcrafted metal animal sculptures. For nearly two decades, master craftsman Moses has been transforming recycled industrial metal into museum-quality wildlife art.

Every piece is conceived, designed, and hand-welded with meticulous attention to detail. We work with reclaimed metal sourced responsibly, transforming it into timeless sculptures that capture the essence and majesty of African wildlife.

Our sculptures grace the lobbies of luxury safari lodges, five-star hotels, international art galleries, and prestigious private collections across East Africa and beyond.

Our Process

🔍

Sourcing

We carefully source reclaimed and recycled metal from industrial sources, selecting only premium materials worthy of fine art.

✏️

Design

Each sculpture begins with detailed sketches and planning, capturing the character and movement of each animal.

🔧

Hand-Welding

Using traditional welding techniques, Moses carefully constructs each piece, shaping and joining metal with precision and artistry.

✨

Finishing

Final shaping, polishing, and protective coating ensure durability and aesthetic perfection for decades of display.
