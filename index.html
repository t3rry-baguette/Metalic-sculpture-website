The Master Artisan

Moses is a skilled welding artisan from Dagoretti, Nairobi, with over 18 years of experience transforming raw metal into fine art. His passion for wildlife and dedication to craftsmanship shine through every sculpture.

Using traditional welding techniques combined with modern artistic vision, Moses hand-crafts each piece from recycled industrial metal. No two sculptures are identical—each carries the unique fingerprint of its maker.

From intimate bird sculptures to monumental lion installations, his work graces luxury safari lodges, international hotels, private collections, and prestigious art galleries across East Africa and beyond.
